Ohrid - The City of Light

Ohrid is one of the oldest settlements in Europe, renowned for its stunning lake and ancient churches. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular destination for history and nature enthusiasts.

Skopje - The Capital

Skopje, the capital of Macedonia, is known for its diverse architecture blending modernity with historical monuments like the Old Bazaar and the Stone Bridge, symbolizing the city’s rich cultural history.

Matka Canyon - Natural Wonder

Matka Canyon offers breathtaking scenery, outdoor activities like hiking and kayaking, and is home to Vrelo Cave, one of the deepest underwater caves in the world, making it a must-visit for adventurers.
